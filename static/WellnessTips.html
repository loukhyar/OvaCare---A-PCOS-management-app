Gentle Lifestyle Shifts to Support Your PCOS Wellness

🍓 Nourishing Nutrition

  • Fill your plate with colorful fruits, veggies, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Choose low-glycemic options to support hormone balance.
  • Add heart-happy fats: avocados, seeds, nuts.
  • Be mindful of added sugars and processed snacks.
  • Your body deserves consistent, caring meals. Don’t skip!

🌸 Empowering Movement

  • Move with love — not pressure. Walk, stretch, dance, flow.
  • Even 10 minutes can lift your mood and calm your mind.
  • Consistency over intensity. Your body will thank you!

💖 Kind Weight Management

  • Focus on feeling strong and vibrant, not numbers.
  • Celebrate your wins, no matter how small.
  • Progress, not perfection — always.

🌙 Stress & Sleep Support

  • Create a calming bedtime routine — your dreams matter.
  • Journaling, deep breathing, cozy lights — simple magic.
  • 7–9 hours of restful sleep is your superpower.

🩺 Caring Medical Support

  • Your health team should support your unique needs — don’t settle for less.
  • Stay informed and advocate for your care confidently.

🤝 Community & Support

  • Surround yourself with those who uplift and understand.
  • You are never alone. Lean on your sisters, your circle, your OvaCare family.

🌼 Smart Supplements (with your doctor’s advice)

  • Inositol may support cycles and reduce insulin resistance.
  • Vitamin D for mood and metabolism — check your levels.
  • Omega-3s for happy hormones and brain clarity.
